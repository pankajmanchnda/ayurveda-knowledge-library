Safety Rules

These rules suppress or narrow suggestions when qualified clinical review is important.

Red flag rule: chest pain, severe breathlessness, fainting, blood in stool or urine, severe abdominal pain, high fever, pregnancy complications, sudden weakness, confusion, uncontrolled vomiting, or severe allergic reaction triggers “Seek medical care promptly” and suppresses herb suggestions.
